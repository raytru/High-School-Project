Why its an issue

If you want to make illegal purchases, it's disturbingly easy to do, products ranging from firearms, drugs, individuals private information, child pornography and other products and substances. Because the deep web isn't indexed by any search engines, it makes it harder for police to track individuals and find certain websites.



Reasons

Due to the deep webs anonymising services, people are able to make these purchases without intervention from law enforcement because of strong encryption
