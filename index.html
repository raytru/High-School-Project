The Hidden World Of The Internet


Deepweb

The internet contains a vast plethora of information that isn't indexed by conventional search engines, that part of the web is known as the Deep Web. Further beyond that is the DarkNet which is a subset of the Deep Web, consisting of overlaying networks that uses public Internet but require distinctive software, configuration, or authorization to access. Which is generally used to hide the identity of the user and intentionally conceal content. The Dark web is predominantly used to conceal criminal or otherwise malicious activities. Anonymizing services which offer strong encryption such a Tor (The Onion Router) allows the exploitation of the Deep Web to conduct illegal and clandestine activities such as illegal transactions which are mainly purchased with digital currencies such as Bitcoin, an anonymous form of money that isn't tied to any banking institution.
